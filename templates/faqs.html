Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate?
Qualifying students in Ireland (see Rules for qualification) between the ages of 13 and 18 are encouraged to participate, and need not have a background in programming.

Do I need to know how to program to participate?
While some of the questions require knowing programming languages, others are based on logic puzzles for which nothing except thinking is required.

How do I sign up without a teacher?
Click here to sign up without a teacher. You will need to download the parental consent form and send it in to Real Nation before you can complete registration.
